About Me

I am VINISH KUMAR born in small village of Haryana. Recently I graduated from University of Ottawa, Canada with masters degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering concentration in Applied Artificial Intelligence where apart from my curriculum studies I dove in the fields of computer science. In the past I did my Bachelors of Engineering from University of Pune, India in elecctronics and telecommunications. I realized how much I had left to learn. A traditional education had given me specialized knowledge and skills but had not set me up with the skills needed to succeed in an environment of constant change.

So, I set out on a journey to answer the question “What knowledge and skills do I need to thrive in the 21st century?”

This is personal portfolio which is a map of my journey. I have documented everything I have learned from years of personal study and conversations with some of the world’s top experts in a range of fields from philosophy to technology and internships and projects.
My experience has taught me that there is a wealth of great information out there …but it can be hard to absorb and convert it into genuine knowledge and understanding that can be applied in daily life. By exploring the podcasts and articles on the site you will gain the knowledge and skills needed to future proof yourself as well as the inspiration and direction you need to take your education into your own hands.
